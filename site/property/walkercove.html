About this home

🏡 THE SPACE
Walker Cove is an easy, single-level lake home on a quiet, wooded cove of Lake Hartwell in Martin, GA. With 4 bedrooms and room to sleep up to 14, the whole group spreads out across open living areas that open onto a big deck and screened porch. A wooded buffer of protected Corps of Engineers land sits between the house and the water - privacy and shade on the walk down to your private dock.

Guests have the entire main level and all the outdoor spaces - the deck, screened porch, firepit, yard, kayaks, and your private dock on Lake Hartwell. Park in the private gravel driveway, with plenty of room for multiple vehicles. (The basement is owner storage and is not part of the rental.)

🎣 ON THE WATER
You're on a calm cove of Lake Hartwell - one of the Southeast's best bass lakes. Cast right off the dock for largemouth, spotted bass, striper, crappie & catfish (try a shaky-head in the dock shade, or cut bait after dark). Holcomb Access ramp is 1.4 miles; Harbor Light Marina - boat rentals + Tilly's Tiki Bar - is a ~20-min ride.

📍 NEARBY
- Toccoa Falls - 186-ft waterfall, short easy walk
- Currahee Mountain - WWII "Band of Brothers" summit drive
- Tugaloo State Park - swim beach, ramps & trails on the lake
- Tallulah Gorge - bucket-list canyon day trip (~1 hr)
- Eat: Appalachian Smokehouse, Gumlog Fish Lodge, Circa 1918 & Calico Store
- Groceries at Ingles in Toccoa

🏡 HOUSE RULES
Pets welcome (fee applies). No parties or events. No smoking. Keep noise down - residential street. Supervise the firepit & put it fully out. Dock lights off at night. The wooded shoreline is protected Corps of Engineers land. The basement is owner storage (no guest access).
