Lake Harbor Shores

Bruce Creek Boat Ramp within 3 miles; fee free to bring your own boat.

C & E Marine rents boats & jet skis!

The fishing is amazing in this cove! Striped bass, Walleye , Crappie, Catfish, & bream are abundant!

Short run by water to Gumlog, Tugaloo State Park, Hartwell State Park.

Harbor Light Marina is about a 20 min boat ride, or the same by car. You can enjoy lunch or dinner at the tiki bar or sit outside overlooking the lake.
