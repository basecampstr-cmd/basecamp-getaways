About this home

🏡 THE SPACE
A rare setup with room for everyone: a single-level 3-bedroom main house PLUS a private detached cottage with its own bedroom and an upstairs game room - ideal for two families, in-laws, or kids who want their own space. Out back, life happens on the water: a hot tub, a covered patio with a grill overlooking the lake, a screened porch with a fireplace for cool evenings, and a dock in good water for swimming and fishing.
