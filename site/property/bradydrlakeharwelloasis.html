Welcome to your spacious 4-bedroom, 3-bathroom lakefront retreat designed for up to 12 guests.

LIVING SPACES:
Sunlit living room with a comfortable pull-out sofa and TV.
Fully equipped kitchen with both a standard coffee maker and a Keurig.
Dining room for group meals.
Sunroom offering panoramic lake views and an additional TV.
Bonus game room with a classic arcade machine and its own TV.

BEDROOMS & BATHS:
Sleeps 12 guests comfortably.
Downstairs Master Bedroom with an en-suite full bathroom (tile walk-in shower).
Downstairs Second Bedroom with access to a full bathroom (tile walk-in shower).

The entire 4-bedroom house.

A spacious back deck with a grill and outdoor dining set.

A fire pit for evening gatherings.
