Welcome to Hiawassee, a charming town nestled in the North Georgia Mountains. Our river front property offers an idyllic, tranquil setting. Outdoor lovers will delight in the waterfront location and many recreational activities available in the area, including kayaking, tubing, and fishing right in the backyard.

Being on the water, and only 16.5 miles from Helen, the best of North Georgia is at your doorstep. This Bavarian Alps themed town of Helen, Georgia is set in the Appalachian mountains near many wineries, the Appalachian trail and fun for the whole family. Experience all this area has to offer, from hiking trails within minutes, to waterparks, tubing (winter and summer), zip lines and ropes courses, horseback riding, picturesque downtowns, wineries, mini-golf, gold/gem mining, breweries and more! Come during late September through the end of October to experience Oktoberfest, one of the longest running in the country, and the changing of the leaves in early fall!

The local wedding venue is only about 2 miles away, making our place a perfect stay for attendees. You can also venture out to explore the quaint town center filled with restaurants, shops, and local attractions.

For grocery needs, there's an ingles a short drive away, ensuring you have everything you need for your stay. The region is known for its breathtaking natural beauty and outdoor adventures, and we're confident that you'll enjoy everything Hiawassee and our riverside retreat have to offer.
