About this home

Welcome to Watersedge on beautiful Lake Hartwell—a pet-friendly, family-ready lake house with newly renovated bathrooms and a brand-new hot tub. Installed EV charger is available for use. The home sleeps 14 guests in beds (plus a queen air mattress for 2 more) and is ideal for multiple families, multi-generational trips, or a relaxed family getaway.

The main-level master suite features a king bed, private bath, and a twin daybed with trundle—perfect for younger kids who want to stay close. Step directly onto the deck from the bedroom and enjoy peaceful lake sunrises. Two additional queen bedrooms are also on the main level (one with a new en-suite bath), along with a full hall bath, fully stocked kitchen, dining area, and a cozy living room.

Kids and teens will love the basement bunk room (sleeps 6) and their own rec room with ping pong, air hockey, board games, and a full-size fridge for snacks and drinks. A second living area and full bathroom complete the lower level.

Outdoor space made for families:
• Screened porch
• Upper deck with remote-controlled awning
• Lower deck with seating, hammock, and hot tub

The private dock sits in a quiet cove

Water toys included:

Need Wi-Fi for school or work? It’s available—but we encourage unplugging for morning fishing, lake days, and evening family game time.

You’re close to everything: groceries and pharmacies 5 minutes away, Clemson University just 20 minutes, yet tucked into a peaceful lake setting.
